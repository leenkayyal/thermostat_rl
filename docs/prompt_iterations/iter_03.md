## Iteration 3

### Prompt used
"Use ONLY the provided metrics and give a tactical HVAC recommendation."

### What failed
The coach sometimes mixed short-term and long-term advice together.
Mid-run alerts became too verbose.

### What changed in next iteration
Separated prompts into:
- mid_run tactical alerts
- end_of_day coaching reports

Limited mid-run alerts to 1-2 sentences.
